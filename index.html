Saylani Welfare International Trust

Pakistan's largest NGO, founded in 1999 by Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui, dedicated to alleviating poverty through extensive free services like food (Saylani Ka Dastarkhwan), health, education (IT training, vocational skills), clean water, and economic empowerment (job banks, interest-free loans). Operating across Pakistan and internationally, Saylani aims to provide basic necessities and opportunities for self-reliance, helping millions daily with a vast network of branches and volunteer support.
